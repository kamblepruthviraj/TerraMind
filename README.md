# 🌱 TerraMind

> AI-Powered Farmer Well-Being, Community Support, and Intelligent Assistance Platform

TerraMind is a comprehensive digital platform designed to support farmers through AI-driven guidance, mental wellness resources, community engagement, and access to government assistance programs. By combining technology with social support, TerraMind helps farmers navigate agricultural, financial, and personal challenges while fostering a stronger and more connected farming community.

---

## 🎯 Vision

To empower farmers with accessible technology that promotes well-being, resilience, and sustainable growth through intelligent support systems and community collaboration.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

- 🤖 AI-Powered Assistant
- 🧠 Stress & Well-Being Assessment
- 👥 Farmer Community Network
- 🏛️ Government Scheme Discovery
- 🚨 Emergency Support Resources
- 📱 Mobile-First Responsive Design
- 🔐 Secure Authentication System
- 📊 Personalized Recommendations
